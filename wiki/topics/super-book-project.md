# Super Book Project (Bible LLM)

**Summary**: An innovative project concept to build a specialized Bible Large Language Model (LLM) and mobile application that acts as a spiritual co-pilot for individuals and families.

**Resource**: [Superbook Kids Website](https://superbook.cbn.com/) | [Google Research Technologies](https://research.google/)

---

## 1. Project Concept

The **Bible LLM** is trained on the open-source King James Version (KJV) Bible, supplemented by transcribed sermons from world evangelists (such as Billy Graham). The application's goal is to offer people guidance, support, and a way to align their daily activities with biblical principles.

## 2. Core Features & Capabilities

- **Daily Co-Pilot**: Interactive guidance from waking up to going to bed (e.g., morning prayer alerts, reading scriptures, and playing customized faith-based songs).
- **Interpersonal & Family Syncing**: Securely connects family members (e.g., husband and wife) to coordinate shared activities without breaching privacy:
  - Reminders for joint prayer (before meals, during lunch/dinner).
  - Works dynamically when spouses are in the same location or remote.
  - Encourages relational health by prompting calls to parents, siblings, and community members.
- **Decision Support**: Analyzes contextual inputs to suggest decisions that promote smooth, biblically aligned interpersonal relations.
- **Drive Mode**: Automatically reads scriptures or plays faith-based stories/songs while commuting (car, train, flight).
- **Children's Content**: Integrates with [Superbook (CBN)](https://superbook.cbn.com/) to teach children using interactive stories and animated biblical adaptations.

## 3. Technology Stack & Security

- **Core Technologies**: Developed within the Google Ecosystem using frameworks from `research.google` and `deepmind.google` for modeling.
- **Privacy & Encryption**: Uses secure connections backed by **Quantum Cryptography** and **RSA Encryption** to guarantee that user data, calls, and chats remain private.
- **Mobile Integration**: requests necessary device permissions (microphone, calls, messages, health apps) to provide real-time audio co-piloting via headphones.

## 4. Ultimate Mission

The ultimate mission is to build an accessible, non-commercial, and spiritually supportive application that guides people globally, helping them construct healthy personal relationships and stay connected with their faith.
