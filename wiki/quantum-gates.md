# Quantum Gates

**Summary**: Operations that change the state of qubits, represented by unitary matrices.

**Sources**: [quantum-mechanics-basics.ipynb](../raw/quantum-mechanics-basics.ipynb), [build-and-run-your-first-quantum-program.ipynb](../raw/build-and-run-your-first-quantum-program.ipynb)

---

Quantum gates are the building blocks of quantum circuits.

## Common Gates
- **Hadamard (H)**: Creates superposition.
- **CNOT (CX)**: Conditional NOT gate, used to create entanglement.
- **Pauli-X**: Equivalent to a classical NOT gate.
