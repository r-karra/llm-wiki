# Qubits

**Summary**: The fundamental unit of quantum information, which can exist in a superposition of states 0 and 1.

**Sources**: [quantum-mechanics-basics.ipynb](../raw/quantum-mechanics-basics.ipynb)

---

Unlike classical bits, qubits can represent 0, 1, or both simultaneously through superposition.

## State Representation
- **Dirac Notation**: $\vert \psi \rangle = \alpha \vert 0 \rangle + \beta \vert 1 \rangle$
- **Vector Notation**: $\begin{pmatrix} \alpha \\ \beta \end{pmatrix}$

## Properties
- **Superposition**: Being in multiple states at once.
- **Entanglement**: Strong correlation between qubits.
