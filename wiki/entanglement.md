# Entanglement

**Summary**: A quantum phenomenon where the states of two or more qubits are linked, such that the state of one cannot be described independently of the others.

**Sources**: [quantum-mechanics-basics.ipynb](../raw/quantum-mechanics-basics.ipynb)

---

Entanglement is a hallmark of quantum computing that allows for complex correlations between qubits.

## Bell State
The Bell state $\vert \Phi^+ \rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} (\vert 00 \rangle + \vert 11 \rangle)$ is a classic example of maximum entanglement.
